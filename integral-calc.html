The Website

This website is a simple, centralized hub of compiled math notes and formulas specifically for Correlation 1 review. Developed as a course requirement for the class of Engr. Neil Jon P. Lotivio, the website's format was directly inspired by his own board exam review strategy of using index-card flashcards. The core philosophy is to eliminate the time-consuming chore of handwriting notes, allowing students to save their energy and focus entirely on actual studying.
